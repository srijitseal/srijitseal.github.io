Media Kit

Srijit Seal is Principal Scientist at Human Chemical Company and Visiting Researcher at Uppsala University, working at the interface of machine learning, cheminformatics, Cell Painting, and drug discovery.

Short Bio

Srijit Seal develops machine learning and cheminformatics approaches for small-molecule bioactivity, cellular morphology profiling, and computational toxicology. His research focuses on practical, reproducible modeling workflows that connect chemical structure, image-based profiles, and biological activity.

Affiliations

Speaking Topics

  • AI/ML for drug discovery and chemical biology
  • Cell Painting and morphology-based profiling
  • Cheminformatics and bioactivity prediction
  • Machine learning for computational toxicology
  • Responsible and reproducible use of AI in scientific workflows

Suggested Talk

AI/ML for Drug Discovery and Cellular Profiling

This talk covers practical machine learning workflows for chemistry and cell morphology data, with emphasis on reproducible modeling, bioactivity prediction, and responsible use of AI in early discovery research.
