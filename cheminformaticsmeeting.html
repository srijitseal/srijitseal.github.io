Broad Institute Journal Club: Inviting Participation and Engagement!

Welcome! Here you can find information about our upcoming Journal Club meeting, which is free to attend and open to all. We start our meeting on August 6th, 2024, at 3:00 PM (ET) with a series of engaging scientific discussions at the Broad Institute Imaging Platform, Cambridge, MA. This session will be held in person, with opportunities for networking afterward.

Presentation Topic:

Machine Learning for Protein Target Prediction

Presenter:

Dea Gogishvili, PhD Candidate, Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam, Faculty of Science, Bioinformatics

Session Schedule:

  • 3:00 PM - 3:30 PM: Presentation by Dea Gogishvili
  • 3:30 PM - 4:00 PM: Response and Discussion with Broad Institute Team

Join us for a thought-provoking session where Dea Gogishvili will share her extensive research on the intersection of machine learning and bioinformatics for protein target prediction. This presentation is set to offer valuable insights and ignite enriching discussions among our team and attendees.
