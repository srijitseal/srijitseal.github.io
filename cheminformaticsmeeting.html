Cambridge Digital Drug Discovery and Cheminformatics Network Meetings

Here you can find information about our upcoming meetings, which is free to attend and open to all. We start our meeting on August 6th, 2024, at 12:00 noon (ET) at the Broad Institute Imaging Platform, Cambridge, MA. This session will be held in person, with opportunities for networking afterward.

Presentation Topic:

AI-driven Exploration of Dementia Biomarkers

Presenter:

Dea Gogishvili, Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam, Faculty of Science, Bioinformatics

Session Schedule:

  • 12:00 Noon- 1:00 PM: Presentation by Dea Gogishvili
  • 1:00 PM - 2:00 PM: Discussions and Networking with Lunch served

Join us for a thought-provoking session where Dea Gogishvili will share her extensive research on the intersection of machine learning and bioinformatics in neurochemistry and dementia research. This presentation is sponsored by the Royal Society of Chemistry and Clare Hall, University of Cambridge.
